About Us

Humble Beginnings

Back in December of 1993, the Taylorboi's Cat Company was formed. 27 and half years later, our staff was hired for their first cat-related business. Since then, Taylorboi's Cat Company has gotten nothing but glowing reviews from satisfied customers.
